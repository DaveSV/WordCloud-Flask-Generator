What Is a Word Cloud?

A word cloud (also known as a tag cloud) is a visual representation of words. Cloud creators are used to highlight popular words and phrases based on frequency and relevance. They provide you with quick and simple visual insights that can lead to more in-depth analyses.

For example, with our algorithm you can define a serch using the significant phrase "Computer programming" and the text will be taken from the relative page from Wikipedia (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_programming)

Algorithm

The set of textual data (words) is obtained from the search for pages on Wikipedia, taking as title value the text entered in the Word Cloud creation form. In this way, an intelligent search is carried out on specific concepts such as personal names, geographical or historical information, trades and techniques, plastic or literary arts, etc.
